About Meal Mapper

Welcome to Meal Mapper, your go-to tool for discovering local ingredients and recipes in Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon! Our app is designed to help residents and visitors alike find fresh, affordable ingredients right in their community, making meal planning easier and more sustainable.

Our Mission

At Meal Mapper, we believe in supporting local farmers, markets, and businesses. By mapping out ingredient locations, prices, and availability, we empower users to make informed decisions, reduce food miles, and enjoy delicious, home-cooked meals using what's available nearby. Whether you're a home cook, a chef, or just someone exploring new recipes, our interactive map and weather updates ensure you're always prepared.

How It Works

Simply select a recipe from our curated list or search for your own. View the ingredients, click on them to see nearby stores and markets on the map, and get real-time weather information to plan your shopping trips. Our app integrates terrain and satellite map views for easy navigation, and we use reliable data sources to keep everything up-to-date.

About the Creators

Meal Mapper was developed by a team of passionate developers and local enthusiasts in Bukidnon. We're committed to promoting the rich agricultural heritage of the region, from the fertile lands of Manolo Fortich to the vibrant public markets. This project started as a way to bridge technology with community needs, and we're excited to grow it with user feedback.
